Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really women only?

Yes! The dorms and common areas are exclusively for women to ensure a comfortable and safe environment.

Is there a kitchen?

Absolutely. We have a fully equipped community kitchen where you can cook your own meals.

How far is the beach?

We are located in a quiet area just a 10-15 minute walk from the main beach and the center of Tamarindo.
